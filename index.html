Chinese Cultural SVG Icons

Jia

Jia. It stands for some big and powerful trees. Its nature is hard and is the material of the pillars.It represents a self-motivated and hard-working person, who is easy to get ahead in a group and is very capable of leadership.

Yi

Yi. It represents flower vine branches and some low plants, which have strong adaptability to the environment and are not easily destroyed.

Bing

Bing. It represents the warmest and warmest things, full of vigor, enthusiasm and cheerfulness.It can forge the hardest metals.

Ding

Ding. It represents a small flame, but it can make people feel hopeful and warm. People feel close and comfortable with it and it is close to people, which is different with Bing.

Wu

Wu. It represents dry soil and cannot be use for growing plant. However, it has useful value for building and construction, so people cannot live without it.

Ji

Ji. It stands for agricultural land, or garden land, which can be used for the cultivation of all plants and giving birth to new life.

Geng

Geng. It represents the hardest and sharpest things on the earth with aggressiveness and perseverance, and represents people with high skills and excellent technology.

Xin

Xin. It represents gorgeous metal and beautiful shape.It also symbol of gentle but principled character, dependable and strategic characteristics.

Ren

Ren. It represents rivers and inclusive things, so it also has a fluid and flexible feeling and stands for very creative and inspirational people.

Gui

Gui. It represents rain and mountain spring water, stream water, etc., so it is the water that is gentle, slow and moisturizing all things. Therefore, it represents a person with a flexible mind, and it also stands for the style of continuous flow.



The 10 icons are inspired by traditional Chinese culture and stem from Book of Changes.The 10 icons symbol the ten most powerful elements. The ten signs are called Jia, Yi, Bing, Ding, Wu, Ji, Geng, Xin, Ren, and Gui. They can also be used in the Chinese lunar calendar method. And they can be used to describe a person's characteristics. For example, if a person was born on Jia day in lunar calendar, he has the same characteristics with some people born on Jia day. The culture is similar with Zodiac system in western countries.
